Mission Statement

At AG Web Design, I believe that people who need a website have a right to find someone who can create a professional-grade website at a fraction of the cost and time of the leading competitors.

You have found that company.

Ideals

Before I begin a project with any customer, I make sure we are on the same page. We determine the price before starting, and we discuss the plans for the finished project.

I believe that a beautiful and unique website can make all the difference for a business. Prospective customers will more than likely not use your business if your website looks like it was carelessly made; they will think that is how you feel about the business, even if that is not the case.

Every website I make will meet these three standards:

  1. The website is easy to navigate and read.
  2. The website is aesthetically pleasing.
  3. The website has all of the needed information for that business.

I also think that the customer asking me to create a website should have a major role in envisioning the site. It is not completely up to me to create a website. I need to know what the customer is wanting the site to do and how they want it to look, otherwise I will not accept the job.

About the Designer

Last year, I was given the opportunity to visit San Francisco, where I toured LinkedIn, Google, and Stanford University. This only reinforced my love of coding, and I am extremely grateful to have seen what advice professionals in the technology industy have to give to people like myself.

I would love to create a website for you!
